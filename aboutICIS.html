The College is located in the heart of Mangaluru city, connected with Central Railway Station offers excellent learning atmosphere. Students are exposed to a variety of curricular, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities that transform them into dynamic change agents who are able to make a difference by their presence wherever they are. Students from different parts of the country and regions who enroll in the college make it truly inclusive and multi-cultural. The college promotes research in relevant fields of Computer and Information Science, offer Ph.D. programme in accordance with the regulations of the University.
