UNDERGRADUATES

BCA Software Development & Web Design

Bachelor of Computer Applications is a 3-year undergraduate program that bridges the gap between IT industries and academic institutes by incorporating the latest Artificial Intelligence technologies into the curriculum. It provides students with a complete understanding of the subject within a structured framework.

BCA Cloud Computing, Ethical Hacking & Cyber Security

This program provides dual career options in Cloud Technology and Information Security. It offers in-depth practical know-how of these trending technologies, in addition to the mandatory subjects of a traditional engineering program.

BCA Artificial Intelligence, Virtual Reality & DevOps

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program offers a specialization in Artificial Intelligence, Virtual Reality, and DevOps. This unique program provides students with the skills and knowledge to excel in these fast-growing technology sectors.


BCA Robotics, Artificial intelligence and virtual reality

The BCA in Robotics, AI, and VR is a 3-year undergraduate program that incorporates the latest AI technologies into the curriculum to bridge the gap between IT industries and academic institutes. It provides students with a complete understanding of these fields within a structured framework.

BCA Full Stack with Cloud Computing & Artificial Intelligence

BCA Full Stack with Cloud Computing & Artificial Intelligence is a specialization program that combines the skills of cloud computing, full-stack development, and artificial intelligence. This program is designed to meet the demands of the industry for fresh graduates.

BCA Data Science, Big Data Analytic & Digital Marketing

BCA Data Science, Big Data Analytic & Digital Marketing is a specialization program that combines the skills of data science, big data analytics, and digital marketing. This program is designed to meet the demands of the industry for fresh graduates and postgraduates. The curriculum covers all important contents of Data Science, Big Data Analytics, and Digital Marketing as per industry demands.


B.Sc. in Animation,Digital FilmMaking & VFX

This program offers Degree Certification in DFM, ANIMATION & VFX. The duration of this program is 3 years (6 Semester). In addition to introducing students to the world of 3D digital art and VFX visualization, this program exposes students to industry relevant software tools.
